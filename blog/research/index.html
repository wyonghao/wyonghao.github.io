Research

Currently I teach DSP and audio networking at Birmingham City University. My research work at the DMT Lab includes high performance digital filter for audio ADC/DAC and Future Network for digital media.

Projects

Future Digital Media Network Architecture

We propose a new layer-2 network architecture to support a TRUE network convergence with manageable and ultra low latency. This network architecture supports audio, video and IP data in one integrated infrastructure.

Low Latency digital filter for ADC/DAC

This project focuses on advanced multistage multirate filter architecture used in oversampling based high resolution audio converters. We are looking into both time domain and frequency domain performance with new hardware efficient cost framework.
